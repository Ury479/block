### **2. GDPR applies to which organizations?** a

GDPR 适用于哪些组织？  
A. Only companies physically located in the EU  
B. Any organization processing EU residents’ personal data  
C. Only government agencies  
D. Only technology companies

Correct answer: **B (Any organization processing EU residents’ personal data)**

Result: **❌ Incorrect**

### ❗ Why your answer is incorrect:

GDPR 不要求企业必须位于 EU 内。  
事实上，即使公司在美国、日本、泰国，只要 **处理 EU 居民的数据**，仍然适用 GDPR。

### ✔ Correct Concept:

GDPR 的适用范围是 _extraterritorial_（域外适用）：

> **任何组织，只要处理 EU residents 的数据，不论是否位于 EU → 都必须遵守 GDPR。**

### **4. Which GDPR principle requires that personal data collected must be limited to what is necessary?** a

哪个原则要求组织只能收集必要的数据？  
A. Integrity and Confidentiality  
B. Purpose Limitation  
C. Data Minimization  
D. Storage Limitation

Correct answer: **C (Data Minimization)**

Result: **❌ Incorrect**

### ❗ Why your answer is incorrect:

- Integrity and Confidentiality（A）指数据安全性
    
- Data Minimization（C）才是“只收集必要数据”
    
- Purpose Limitation（B）是“不得改变收集目的”
    
### ✔ Correct Concept:

**Data Minimization = 只处理必要的数据，不多收，不滥收。**  
这是 GDPR 的核心原则之一。

### **5. Which role is responsible for determining the purposes and means of processing personal data?** c

哪个角色负责决定数据处理的目的和方式？  
A. Data Processor  
B. Data Controller  
C. DPO  
D. CIO

Correct answer: **B (Data Controller)**

Result: **❌ Incorrect**

### ❗ Why your answer is incorrect:

DPO（选项 C）**不负责决定数据处理目的**，其作用是监督、咨询、确保合规。

决定目的与方式的人是：

### ✔ **Data Controller**

例如：

- 银行决定如何使用客户信息 → 银行是 controller
    
- 云服务公司代银行处理数据 → 云服务是 processor

2. Which of the following is NOT a typical source for estimating likelihood of exploitation?  
    下列哪一项不是估计被攻击可能性的典型信息来源？   b
    A. Past incidents  
    B. Industry guidelines  
    C. Expert opinions  
    D. User satisfaction surveys
    
    Correct answer: D  
Result: ❌ Incorrect  
Explanation:  
Typical sources for estimating likelihood include past incidents, industry guidelines, and expert opinions. User satisfaction surveys do not provide any insight into cybersecurity threats or exploitation likelihood. Industry guidelines are indeed legitimate sources and therefore not the correct choice. The right answer is “user satisfaction surveys,” as they are unrelated to risk likelihood assessment.

3. Which vulnerability category includes coding bugs and unprotected communication channels?  
    下列哪类弱点包括编码缺陷和未受保护的通信渠道？   a
    A. Behavioral  
    B. Misinterpretation  
    C. Coding problems  
    D. Physical vulnerabilities
    
    Correct answer: C  
Result: ❌ Incorrect  
Explanation:  
Coding problems refer to security weaknesses caused by defective software, poorly defined requirements, insecure communication channels, and programming errors. These are technical vulnerabilities.  
Behavioral vulnerabilities, on the other hand, involve employee mistakes or improper processes, not coding issues. Therefore, “coding problems” is the correct choice for vulnerabilities involving coding bugs and unprotected communication channels.
